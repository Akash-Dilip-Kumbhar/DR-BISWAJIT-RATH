About Me

I am a veteran AI and Data Transformation leader who believes that the true power of Artificial Intelligence lies not in replacing humans but in amplifying human intelligence, empathy and judgment.

I bring over twenty-five years of experience across industries including telecom, BFSI, chemicals, fashion, and engineering, working at the intersection of technology, data, and business. My academic journey includes an M.Tech and MBA in Information Technology, a Ph.D. in Behavioural Analytics Management, and an Executive General Management program from IIM Lucknow. Over the years, my work has been recognised with several industry honours, including Chief Analytics Officer of the Year (2025), Technology Leader of the Year (2024), and GenAI Leader of the Year (2024), among others that reflect my commitment to building practical, impactful innovation.
